You are designing and building a clean, professional, and user-friendly "Industry Norms & Deemed NPAT" section for a modern B-BBEE compliance web application. This section is used to look up quarterly industry profit margin norms (from Stats SA P0044) and calculate Deemed NPAT — which directly affects ESD and SED targets (2% SD, 1% ED, 1% SED of NPAT).

Goal:
Make this section feel like a smart, reliable lookup tool + calculator. Users should quickly select their industry and date, see the correct norm, understand how Deemed NPAT is derived, and see the impact on their scorecard — all in one glance. It should be intuitive even for non-finance people (transformation managers, HR, consultants).

Core Requirements for the UI Section:

1. Overall Layout & Style
   - Full-width card or dedicated tab/page called "Industry Norms & Deemed NPAT"
   - Clean, professional design: white/light background, blue accents for interactive elements, green for positive/calculated values
   - Responsive: desktop shows side-by-side panels, mobile stacks vertically
   - Three main zones:
     A. Top: Quick selector + current norm summary (hero card)
     B. Middle: Interactive table of norms (filterable/sortable)
     C. Bottom: Deemed NPAT calculator + impact preview

2. Top Section – Quick Selector & Summary Card (Hero)
   - Big title: "Industry Norms Lookup & Deemed NPAT Calculator"
   - Two main dropdowns side-by-side (or stacked on mobile):
     - Industry: searchable dropdown with ~10 options (All industries, Mining, Manufacturing, Electricity Gas & Water, Construction, Trade, Transport & Communication, Real Estate & Business Services, Community/Social/Personal Services)
     - Publication Date / Quarter: dropdown showing latest first (e.g. "Q2 2025 – 27 Jun 2025", "Q4 2024 – 31 Dec 2024", etc.)
   - Auto-show summary card below dropdowns:
     - Selected Industry: [name]
     - Selected Norm Date: [date]
     - Industry Norm Margin: [X.XX%] (big bold green number)
     - Status badge: "Latest Available" (green) or "Historical" (grey) or "Negative Margin – using 0% floor" (orange warning)
     - Small note: "Source: Stats SA P0044 Quarterly Financial Statistics | Last refreshed: [app date]"

3. Middle Section – Norms History Table
   - Collapsible accordion or tab: "View All Historical Norms"
   - Interactive table (use TanStack Table / AG Grid / MUI DataGrid):
     - Columns: Publication Date | Industry | Norm Margin (%) | Notes (e.g. "Negative – floored at 0%")
     - Sortable by date (newest first default) and by norm value
     - Filterable: by industry, by year, search box for quick find
     - Highlight row matching current selection (bold + light blue background)
     - Pagination: 10–20 rows per page (data has ~100+ entries)
     - Export button: "Download Full Norms CSV" (excel-friendly)

4. Bottom Section – Deemed NPAT Calculator & Impact Preview
   - Card titled: "Deemed NPAT Calculation for ESD/SED Targets"
   - Inputs (pre-filled where possible):
     - Your Revenue (ZAR): number input (pull from Financials section if available)
     - Your Actual NPAT (ZAR): number input (pull from Financials)
     - Your Actual NPAT Margin (%): auto-calculated
     - 25% of Industry Norm: auto-shown (e.g. if norm 5.76% → 1.44%)
   - Logic display (step-by-step, readable):
     Step 1: Compare actual margin vs 25% of norm
     Step 2: If actual margin ≥ 25% of norm → Use Actual NPAT
     Step 3: Else → Deemed NPAT = Revenue × Industry Norm
     Result: Big number "Deemed NPAT = R XXX,XXX,XXX" (green if using deemed, blue if actual)
   - Impact preview mini-cards:
     - ESD (Supplier Dev) Target: 2% of Deemed NPAT → R XXXk (points potential 10)
     - ED Target: 1% of Deemed NPAT → R XXXk (points potential 5)
     - SED Target: 1% of Deemed NPAT → R XXXk (points potential 5)
   - Warning if negative norm: "Negative industry norm detected – using 0% floor for deemed calculation"

6. Integration Points

7. UX Polish & Cool Touches
